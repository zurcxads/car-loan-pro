# Auto Loan Pro Brand Guidelines — Option C: Bold & Disruptive

## Brand Story & Positioning

Auto Loan Pro is here to disrupt the auto lending industry. We're tired of seeing consumers get ripped off by dealership finance managers and buried in fine print by traditional banks. We're building a radically transparent, consumer-first marketplace that puts power back where it belongs — in your hands.

**Positioning:** "Get your money first, then go shopping"

**Brand Promise:** The most transparent, fastest, and boldest way to finance your vehicle. No BS. No hidden fees. Just better deals.

**Target Audience:** Adults 25-55, all credit tiers, seeking new or used vehicle financing (with emphasis on younger, tech-native consumers)

**Brand Personality:**
- Bold & Confident
- Disruptive & Different
- Transparent & Direct
- Unapologetically Consumer-First

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
**Text-only wordmark:** "AUTO LOAN PRO"

**Typography:** All caps, tight letter-spacing for impact
**Treatment:** Optional gradient text effect (electric blue to purple)
**Font:** Outfit or Sora (geometric sans-serif)
**Minimum size:** 100px width
**Clear space:** Minimum 20px on all sides

### Logo Variations
1. **Primary:** Gradient text (blue to purple) on dark background
2. **Reverse:** White all-caps on dark
3. **Monochrome:** Electric blue all-caps
4. **Light backgrounds:** Near-black text
5. **Dark backgrounds:** White or gradient text

### Spacing Rules
- Logo clear space = height of capital "A"
- All-caps treatment is mandatory
- Tight tracking (letter-spacing: -0.02em)
- Never use mixed case
- Never add serifs or decorative elements
- Gradient optional but preferred for hero moments

## Typography

### Typeface System

**Headings & UI:** Outfit or Sora (geometric sans-serif)
- Bold, modern, geometric letterforms
- Use for: Headlines, buttons, UI elements, brand moments
- Weights: 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold), 800 (extrabold)

**Body Text:** Inter (sans-serif)

- Use for: Body copy, paragraphs, form labels

## Photography & Imagery Direction

### Photography Style
- **Mood:** Bold, energetic, confident, night/dusk preferred
- **Subjects:** Confident individuals (25-40), modern vehicles, urban settings
- **Lighting:** Dramatic, neon-lit, moody but not dark
- **Composition:** Dynamic angles, movement, energy

### What to Show
- People confidently holding keys
- Modern cars in urban/night settings
- Close-ups of digital interfaces (phones, tablets)
- Celebrating success moments
- Neon-lit dealerships or city streets
- Action shots (walking with confidence)

### What to Avoid
- Boring daylight photography
- Traditional bank imagery
- Stuffy corporate environments
- Overly posed stock photos
- Luxury-only vehicles

### Image Treatment
- High contrast
- Slightly desaturated with neon accent pops
- Can use color grading (blue/cyan/purple tones)
- Vignettes acceptable
- Glow effects on highlights

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Brand Voice

**Core Attributes:**
- **Bold:** We say what others won't. Direct and unfiltered.
- **Confident:** We know we're better. We own it.
- **Transparent:** Zero BS. We tell it like it is.
- **Empowering:** You're in control. We're just the weapon.

### Tone by Context

**Marketing & Landing Pages:**
- Aggressive and confident
- Challenge the status quo
- Use power words and contrasts
- Example: "Stop getting screwed by dealerships. Get your money first. Walk in like you own the place."

**Application Flow:**
- Direct and fast
- Remove friction, show speed
- Celebrate every step
- Example: "That's it. You're already halfway there. Let's finish this."

**Results & Offers:**
- Victorious and bold
- Make them feel like they won
- Clear, simple next steps
- Example: "You just beat the system. Here are 12 lenders fighting for your business."

**Support & Help:**
- Straight-talking but helpful
- No corporate speak
- Use bullet points, be clear
- Example: "Got a question? Here's the real answer:"

### Writing Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use active, punchy sentences
- Be direct. Cut the fluff.
- Use contractions freely
- Write like you talk
- Challenge assumptions
- Use "you" and "we"
- Use fragments for emphasis. Like this.

**Don't:**
- Use corporate jargon
- Dance around the point
- Sound like a bank
- Use exclamation points for fake excitement
- Write long paragraphs
- Be overly formal

### Vocabulary

**Prefer:**
- "Beat the system" over "get a good deal"
- "You're in control" over "we help you"
- "Zero BS" over "transparent"
- "Get your money" over "receive financing"
- "Walk in like you own the place" over "shop with confidence"

## Do's and Don'ts

### Logo

✅ **DO:**
- Use all-caps consistently
- Maintain tight letter-spacing
- Use gradient on dark backgrounds
- Keep it bold and impactful

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use mixed case
- Add taglines directly to logo
- Use light weights
- Place on busy backgrounds

### Color

✅ **DO:**
- Embrace dark backgrounds
- Use neon accents sparingly
- Create glow effects on interactive elements
- Maintain high contrast

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use pastel colors
- Overuse gradients (reserve for hero moments)
- Mix too many neon colors at once
- Use light gray backgrounds

### Typography

✅ **DO:**
- Use all-caps for impact moments
- Use Outfit/Sora for headlines
- Use Inter for body text
- Embrace tight letter-spacing

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use serif fonts
- Use light font weights on dark backgrounds
- Set long paragraphs in all-caps
- Use decorative fonts

### Imagery

✅ **DO:**
- Use dramatic, high-contrast photography
- Embrace night/dusk shots
- Show confident, diverse people
- Use neon color grading

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use boring stock photos
- Use traditional corporate imagery
- Show only luxury vehicles
- Use flat, daylight-only photography

## Dark Mode Palette

This brand is **dark-mode first**. Light mode is secondary.

### Light Mode Adjustments (if needed)

**Background**
- Hex: `#F4F4F5` (Zinc 100)
- Usage: Primary light background

**Surface**
- Hex: `#FFFFFF`
- Usage: Cards on light background

**Text Primary**
- Hex: `#09090B` (Zinc 950)
- Usage: Headlines, primary text

**Text Secondary**
- Hex: `#3F3F46` (Zinc 700)
- Usage: Body text, captions

**Borders**
- Hex: `#E4E4E7` (Zinc 200)
- Usage: Dividers, card borders

### Light Mode Rules

1. Reduce glow effects (they don't work on light backgrounds)
2. Use solid electric blue instead of gradients
3. Increase border contrast
4. Maintain neon green for success states
5. Test all interactive states

**Note:** This brand is strongest on dark backgrounds. Light mode should be used sparingly.

## Implementation Notes

This brand direction emphasizes **disruption, confidence, and boldness** through:
- Dark-first design with neon accents
- All-caps typography (Outfit/Sora) for impact
- Glow effects and high contrast
- Dramatic, energetic photography
- Direct, challenging voice and tone

**Best for:** Differentiating from all traditional lenders, appealing to younger/tech-savvy consumers, making a statement in a boring industry.

**Consider this option if:** You want to be the Robinhood/Cash App of auto lending — bold, disruptive, and unapologetically different. This brand says "we're here to change the game."
